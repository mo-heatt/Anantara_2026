Rules & Scoring System

Core Regulations

  • Compulsory Participation: Participation in all events is mandatory. Failure to participate in any event will result in a deduction of 1000 points.
  • Time Limit: Exceeding the time limit for any event leads to a deduction of 200 points for that event.

Bonus & Joker Rules

  • Early Bird Points (per day): Full team arrival at the venue earns:
    8:00 AM – 8:30 AM: +1000 points 8:30 AM – 9:00 AM: +500 points
  • Joker Placement: Joker must be placed at the registration counter during registration and can be used only on a Supreme Tier event, specifying the exact position (1st / 2nd / 3rd).
  • Joker Scoring: If the team wins the specified position: +2000 points. If they fail to win that position, their score for that event becomes 0, irrespective of any other position.
